## About me

I am a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence with a strong interest in **computer vision, medical image segmentation, and applied deep learning**.  
Alongside AI research, I also enjoy building **full-stack web products** that are practical, clean, and user-centered.

My work usually sits between two worlds:

- building and evaluating **AI models with PyTorch**
- turning ideas into usable **software products with modern web stacks (e.g., Next.js)**

So if it involves **vision models, product thinking, and shipping real things**, I am probably interested.
